Who can use this Service?

You can only use this service if any of the following apply:

  • You are aged 55 or above
  • You have previously received a letter saying that you are at high risk from coronavirus.
  • You are an eligible frontline health worker

If you are not eligible yet:

Wait to be contacted. We will let you know when its your turn to have the vaccine.

  • get the 2nd dose 11 to 12 weeks after getting your 1st dose

About Us

We want to make vaccination easy and accessible to everyone.

VaccineFinder is a free, online service where users can search for locations that offer vaccinations. We work with partners such as clinics, pharmacies, and health departments to provide accurate and up-to-date information about vaccination services. VaccineFinder is operated by epidemiologists and software developers at Boston Children's Hospital.
